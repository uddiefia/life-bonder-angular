Team

Team Rockstar Warriors

We are a team of volunteering enthusiasts from all over the world that share the same vision: a social media platform that lives up to the promise of what social media can be: A tool to facilitate human connections rather than replacing them. We want to make the world a better place by filling a growing need - connecting people in friendships that are human, profound and meaningful. LifeBonder is a belief that genuine friendship is something we can all achieve. Something we all deserve!
